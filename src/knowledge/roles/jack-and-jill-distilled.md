these literally into polished content without cleanup:

- `Deuterium blockchain` -> likely intended to mean Ethereum
- `Podantic's LogFire` -> likely Pydantic Logfire
- `thought code` -> likely a mistaken transcript of another tool name
- `OpenClawed` -> likely OpenCode or a similar tool

Also avoid overusing slang like:

- `agentic engineering pilled`
- `vibe coding`

Those may be fine in raw notes, but not ideal default wording for the
conversational CV unless used sparingly and intentionally.
